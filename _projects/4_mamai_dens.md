**Role:** Data Scientist (Oct 2024 – Present).
**Funding:** FONDEF IT23I0040 (ANID Chile).

**mamAI-Dens** develops deep learning models for the **automatic segmentation and quantification of dense breast tissue** in digital mammography images. Breast density is a known risk factor and a confounder for cancer detection, and reliable, reproducible measurement is a key step toward personalized screening.

I contribute to:

- Model design and benchmarking for tissue segmentation.
- Dataset preparation and anonymization (in collaboration with Hospital Carlos Van Buren).
- Evaluation of clinical applicability and reproducibility.
