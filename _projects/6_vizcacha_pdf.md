**Role:** Project Director (Sept 2025 – Present).
**Context:** Second edition of the Engineering 2030 Prototyping Contest, Universidad de Valparaíso.

**VIZCACHA-PDF** uses **Large Language Models** to perform **semantic extraction and standardization of clinical information from PDF documents** — turning unstructured radiology reports, clinical histories, and exam metadata into structured, queryable data.

The project complements the imaging side of VIZCACHA by tackling the *text* layer of the clinical record: it aims to build a unified, structured representation of patient information that downstream AI tools (segmentation, conversational chat, decision support) can rely on.
