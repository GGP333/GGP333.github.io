**Role:** Data Scientist (Sept 2025 – Present).
**Funding:** ANID Chile, project VIU25P0233.

**VIZCACHA** is a translational AI project developed at Universidad de Valparaíso that combines:

- **Explainable segmentation** of brain tumors on multi-contrast MRI.
- **Large Language Models** for assisted clinical chat over imaging studies.
- A pipeline aimed at producing prototype tools that can ultimately fit into Chilean radiology workflows.

The project is a direct continuation of my research line on explainable medical AI, with a stronger focus on usability and translation to real clinical environments.
