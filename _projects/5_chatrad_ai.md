**Role:** Project Director (Nov 2024 – Jun 2025).
**Context:** First edition of the Engineering 2030 Prototyping Contest, Universidad de Valparaíso.

**ChatRad AI** explored how **Large Language Models and agentic systems** can streamline interactions with **RIS (Radiology Information Systems) and PACS (Picture Archiving and Communication Systems)**. The prototype demonstrated assisted querying, structured reporting, and contextual support over imaging metadata using natural language.

This work was the seed of my ongoing **ChatRad AI–Brain** MSc thesis, which extends the same conversational-radiology vision toward brain tumor MRI segmentation with explainability built in.
