**Role:** Lead researcher · MSc Thesis (Universidad de Valparaíso, in progress).

**ChatRad AI–Brain** integrates **explainable segmentation models** for brain tumors with a **conversational interface powered by Large Language Models and agents**. The goal is to give radiologists a single tool that:

1. Performs robust segmentation on multi-contrast MRI.
2. Surfaces *why* the model predicted what it predicted via attention-based, quantitatively evaluated explainability.
3. Lets clinicians interrogate, compare and document studies in natural language.

This thesis builds on my undergraduate Swin UNETR work and extends it toward **quantitative evaluation of visual explainability** — moving beyond qualitative heatmap inspection toward rigorous, reproducible XAI metrics suited for clinical validation.
